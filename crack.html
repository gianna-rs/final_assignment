Christmas Crack

Makes approx. 24 pieces

This salty and sweet candy treat is deliciously addictive! Piece after piece, you'll be enjoying this until Christmas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 package salted soda crackers (like Premium Plus)
  • chopped nuts of choice
  • 1 package (approx. 300 grams) milk chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Line a cookie sheet with crackers, salted side facing up. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Leave mixture on medium heat until mixture boils. Do not stir! Let boil for about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and add vanilla.
  5. Pour the mixture quickly over the crackers. Bake for 5 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top of crackers, let sit for a few minutes - enough time for the chocolate to melt. Spread the chocolate evenly and sprinkle with chopped nuts of choice.
  7. Place in refrigerator and let cool completely. Once cooled, break apart into desired size of pieces.
